Praeto runs on a fully player-driven economy where diamonds are the universal currency. Every action — trading at shops, bidding at auctions, wagering in prediction markets, or upgrading your membership — flows through a single diamond wallet tied to your account.

## The Core Loop

The Praeto economy follows a straightforward cycle that rewards active players:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Earn Diamonds">
    Mine diamonds underground, sell goods to other players, or win prediction market wagers to grow your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Spend at Shops & Auctions">
    Use your diamonds to buy items from player-run shops or place bids in the auction house.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wager in Prediction Markets">
    Put diamonds on the line in Praeto's prediction markets. Correct calls multiply your stake; wrong ones cost you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Buy Memberships">
    Spend diamonds on a membership tier to unlock weekly credits, exclusive perks, and expanded access to platform features.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your Diamond Wallet

Every Praeto player gets a diamond wallet the moment they join. Your wallet tracks:

- **Total diamonds** — the running balance available to spend right now
- **Weekly credits** — bonus diamonds granted by your membership tier each week
- **Used credits** — how many of this week's credits you have already spent
- **Spending history** — a full log of every transaction your account has made

Your account is identified by both your Discord UUID and your Minecraft username (`mc_name`), so your wallet stays in sync across the platform and in-game.

## Transaction Types

Praeto records every diamond movement automatically. Common transaction kinds include:

| Kind | When It Happens |
|---|---|
| `membership_purchase` | You buy or renew a membership tier |
| `shop_purchase` | You buy an item from a player shop |
| `auction_bid` | You place or win a bid in the auction house |
| `wager` | You stake diamonds in a prediction market |
| `wager_payout` | You receive winnings from a correct prediction |

<Info>
  Credits reset every week at the time shown in your balance display. Unspent credits do **not** roll over — use them before the reset to get the most from your membership.
</Info>

## Weekly Credit Resets

Membership tiers grant weekly credits on a rolling schedule. Your `week_end_at` timestamp tells you exactly when your current credit window closes. Once the reset fires, your `used_credits` counter clears and a fresh batch of credits is added to your wallet.

<Tip>
  Check your reset time before the end of the week and plan any shop purchases or auction bids around it. Spending credits just before a reset maximises the value you extract from your membership.
</Tip>
