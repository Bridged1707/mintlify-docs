Every diamond that enters or leaves your wallet generates a transaction record. Praeto keeps a complete, auditable log of your economic activity — from membership purchases to auction wins and prediction market payouts.

## Viewing Your Transaction History

Run the following command in-game to open your transaction log:

```
/wallet history
```

The log lists your most recent transactions in reverse chronological order. Each entry shows the transaction kind, the diamond amount, and the timestamp.

To narrow results to a specific type, pass the kind as an argument:

```
/wallet history <kind>
```

For example:

```
/wallet history membership_purchase
/wallet history wager
```

## Transaction Types

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| `membership_purchase` | Diamonds spent to buy or renew a membership tier |
| `shop_purchase` | Diamonds spent buying items from a player-run shop |
| `shop_sale` | Diamonds received when another player buys from your shop |
| `auction_bid` | Diamonds held or spent when you place or win an auction bid |
| `auction_refund` | Diamonds returned when you are outbid in the auction house |
| `wager` | Diamonds staked on a prediction market event |
| `wager_payout` | Diamonds received as winnings from a correct prediction |
| `deposit` | Physical diamonds deposited from inventory into your wallet |
| `withdrawal` | Wallet balance converted back into physical diamonds |

<Info>
  Credits spent via your weekly allowance are tracked separately from raw diamond transactions but appear in the same history log with a `credit` prefix on the kind field (for example, `credit_shop_purchase`).
</Info>

## Reading a Transaction Entry

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the Kind">
    The `kind` field tells you what triggered the transaction — a shop purchase, a wager, a membership renewal, and so on. Use this to quickly categorise what you are reviewing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the Amount">
    Positive amounts represent diamonds entering your wallet (sales, payouts, deposits). Negative amounts represent diamonds leaving your wallet (purchases, wagers, withdrawals).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Note the Timestamp">
    Each transaction is stamped with the exact date and time it occurred. Use timestamps to cross-reference purchases with in-game events or Discord bot interactions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the Running Balance">
    Your balance after each transaction is displayed alongside the entry, making it easy to spot unexpected drops or unusual activity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Transaction Scenarios

<Accordion title="Membership purchase">
  When you buy a membership tier, a `membership_purchase` entry appears with a negative diamond amount equal to the tier cost. Immediately after, your `weekly_credits` allocation updates to reflect the new tier's credit grant.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Prediction market wager and payout">
  Placing a wager creates a `wager` entry that debits your wallet. If your prediction is correct, a `wager_payout` entry credits your wallet with the original stake plus winnings. If incorrect, no payout entry is created — the wager entry is the final record.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Outbid at the auction house">
  When someone outbids you, an `auction_refund` entry returns your held diamonds. Your net position returns to what it was before your bid, so you are never permanently out of pocket for a losing bid.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Shop sale while offline">
  Player shops operate while you are offline. When a buyer purchases from your shop, a `shop_sale` entry is created and the diamonds are added to your wallet immediately — even if you are not logged in.
</Accordion>

<Tip>
  Review your transaction history at the end of each week, just before your credit reset. Totalling up your `wager` and `shop_purchase` entries gives you a clear picture of where your diamonds are going and helps you decide where to cut back or double down in the next week.
</Tip>

## Exporting Your History

If you need a full record for budgeting or dispute resolution, use the export command to receive a summary via the Praeto Discord bot:

```
/wallet export
```

The bot sends you a formatted message listing your last 30 days of transactions directly in your Discord DMs.

<Warning>
  Transaction records are retained for 90 days. If you need to reference activity older than that, export your history before the retention window closes.
</Warning>
