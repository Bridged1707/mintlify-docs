Praeto is a Minecraft SMP (Survival Multiplayer) platform built around a rich player-driven economy. Using diamonds as currency, you can run your own shop, trade at auction, track live item prices, place wagers on prediction markets, and unlock weekly credits through membership tiers — all tied seamlessly to your Discord account.

## How Praeto Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Join the Praeto Minecraft server and link your Discord account to activate your player profile and diamond wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn diamonds">
    Mine, trade, and participate in server events to accumulate diamonds — the universal currency on Praeto.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade on the marketplace">
    Open your own chest shop, list items at auction, and monitor live item prices to buy low and sell high.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wager on prediction markets">
    Use your diamonds to place wagers on server events — from PvP outcomes to economy milestones — and earn more when you're right.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  New to Praeto? Head to the [Account Setup](/getting-started/account-setup) guide to get your player profile configured and your Discord linked before your first session.
