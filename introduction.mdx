Praeto is a Minecraft Survival Multiplayer platform that turns your in-game diamonds into real economic power. This page introduces the core features of the platform, explains how the player-driven economy works, and helps you understand whether Praeto is the right server for you.

## What Makes Praeto Different

Most Minecraft servers treat diamonds as crafting materials. On Praeto, diamonds **are** the currency. Every transaction, every shop listing, every market wager — all of it runs on a single unified diamond economy that players control from the ground up. There are no admin-spawned riches, no pay-to-win shortcuts: only the diamonds you mine, trade, and earn.

## Who Is Praeto For?

Praeto is built for Minecraft players who want more than survival — players who enjoy the strategy of a real economy alongside the creativity of building and exploring. You'll get the most out of Praeto if you:

- Want to **trade and speculate** on item prices using live market data
- Enjoy **running a business** through your own player shop
- Like **competitive prediction** — putting diamonds on the line for server events
- Value a **transparent, auditable** economy where every transaction is on record

<Note>
  You'll need a valid Minecraft Java Edition account to join Praeto. Economy features — including your diamond wallet and marketplace access — require a linked Discord account. See [Link Your Discord Account](/getting-started/discord-link) to get set up.
</Note>
