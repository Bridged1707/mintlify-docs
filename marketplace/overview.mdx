The Praeto marketplace is entirely player-driven: every price, every listing, and every bid comes from real players on the server. Whether you want to sell a stack of logs or corner the diamond market, the marketplace gives you the tools to trade smart.

## Three Ways to Trade

There are three distinct ways to participate in the Praeto economy:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Chest Shops" icon="store" href="marketplace/shops">
    Buy or sell items at fixed diamond prices using player-owned chest shops. Browse shops in-game and trade instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Auctions" icon="gavel" href="marketplace/auctions">
    List items for timed auction and let the market decide the price. Place diamond bids and claim your winnings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Item Prices" icon="chart-line" href="marketplace/item-prices">
    Track live market prices powered by VWAP and market candles. Use price history to trade at the right moment.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How the Economy Works

All transactions on Praeto use **diamonds** as the base currency. There is no admin-set pricing — supply, demand, and player activity determine every market rate.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Earn diamonds">
    Mine, trade, or win diamonds through gameplay. Diamonds are your purchasing power across the entire marketplace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find what you need">
    Browse chest shops for instant fixed-price trades, or check active auctions for rare or bulk items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the price data">
    Before buying or listing, look up the current VWAP for an item to make sure you're getting a fair deal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade and profit">
    Complete your transaction. Sold items and won auctions transfer automatically — diamonds move between players in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Marketplace at a Glance

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Currency** | Diamonds |
| **Shops** | Fixed-price chest shops owned and stocked by players |
| **Auctions** | Timed listings with open diamond bidding |
| **Price tracking** | VWAP (volume-weighted average price) + market candles |
| **Asset families** | Related item forms grouped for easier price comparison |

<Info>
  All market data — prices, auction results, and shop listings — reflects actual player transactions. There is no bot activity or admin intervention in pricing.
</Info>

## Getting Started

If you're new to the Praeto marketplace, start with chest shops: they're the fastest way to buy items at a known price with no bidding required. Once you're comfortable, explore auctions for better deals on rare items and check the item price pages to sharpen your trading strategy.
