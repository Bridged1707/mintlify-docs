Praeto membership gives you a recurring weekly allowance of credits alongside exclusive server perks — all purchased entirely with in-game diamonds, no real money required. Whether you want faster access to the marketplace, priority shop listings, or simply more credits to spend on premium server activities each week, a membership tier is the fastest way to level up your gameplay.

## Why Get a Membership?

Membership tiers unlock three core benefits that free players cannot access:

## How Membership Works

Membership is a weekly subscription paid entirely in diamonds. When you purchase or renew a tier, diamonds are deducted from your in-game balance and your credit wallet is loaded with that tier's weekly credit allowance. Your credits then reset automatically every week at the time shown in your wallet's **Resets At** timestamp.

<Note>
  Membership is purchased using **in-game diamonds** — not real-world currency. Make sure your diamond balance is sufficient before initiating a purchase or renewal.
</Note>

## Check Your Membership Status

You can view your current membership tier and remaining credits at any time directly in-game. Your credit wallet displays:

- Your **current tier** (Bronze, Silver, or Gold)
- Your **weekly credit allowance** granted by that tier
- Your **credits used** so far this week
- Your **credits remaining** (`weekly_credits − used_credits`)
- The **reset timestamp** (`week_end_at`) when your credits refresh
