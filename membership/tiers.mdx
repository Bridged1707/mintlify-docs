Praeto offers three membership tiers — Bronze, Silver, and Gold — each providing a progressively larger weekly credit allowance and a richer set of server perks. Upgrading your tier is a one-time diamond purchase that immediately refreshes your credit wallet and unlocks the perks associated with your new tier.

## Tier Comparison

| Tier | Weekly Credits | Key Perks |
|------|---------------|-----------|
| **Bronze** | Low | Basic weekly credits, marketplace access |
| **Silver** | Medium | More weekly credits, priority shop listings |
| **Gold** | High | Maximum credits, all perks unlocked |

<Tip>
  Diamond costs scale with tier level — Bronze is the most affordable entry point, while Gold requires a significantly larger diamond investment. Plan your in-game economy accordingly before committing to a higher tier.
</Tip>

## Tier Details

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Bronze">
    Bronze is the entry-level membership tier, giving you a modest weekly credit allowance and access to the core membership marketplace. It's the ideal starting point if you want to experience paid perks without a heavy diamond commitment.

    **What you get:**
    - A weekly credit allowance to spend on premium server activities
    - Access to the members-only marketplace
    - Basic priority over free players for high-demand features
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Silver">
    Silver doubles down on the Bronze experience with a larger weekly credit allowance and priority shop listings that put your trades in front of more players.

    **What you get:**
    - A medium weekly credit allowance (more than Bronze)
    - Priority shop listings in the marketplace
    - All Bronze perks included
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Gold">
    Gold is the top-tier membership, delivering the maximum weekly credit allowance and unlocking every perk available on the server. If you play Praeto daily and want the best possible experience, Gold is for you.

    **What you get:**
    - Maximum weekly credit allowance
    - All server perks fully unlocked
    - Highest priority across all marketplace and premium features
    - All Bronze and Silver perks included
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to Purchase or Upgrade a Membership

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your diamond balance">
    Open your in-game inventory or use the balance command to confirm you have enough diamonds. Higher tiers require more diamonds, so make sure your balance covers the full cost before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the membership menu">
    Run the membership purchase command in-game to open the tier selection interface. You'll see the diamond cost and weekly credit amount displayed for each tier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your desired tier">
    Choose Bronze, Silver, or Gold. The interface will show you exactly how many diamonds will be deducted from your balance before you confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the purchase">
    Confirm your selection. Diamonds are immediately deducted and your credit wallet is loaded with the new tier's weekly allowance. If you're upgrading from an existing tier, your credits refresh instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your new tier">
    Check your membership status in-game to confirm the tier change. Your updated weekly credit allowance and new perks should be active immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Check Your Current Tier

Run the membership status command at any time to see:

- Your **active tier** (Bronze, Silver, or Gold)
- Your **weekly credit allowance** for that tier
- The **diamond cost** to upgrade to the next tier
- Your credit wallet's current **reset timestamp**

<Info>
  If your membership lapses — for example, if you don't renew before the weekly reset — your credit wallet reverts to zero and membership perks are suspended until you purchase again.
</Info>
